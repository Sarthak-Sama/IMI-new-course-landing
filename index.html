How is this Programme Comparable to an MBA?

In India, understanding the equivalency and credibility of management qualifications is crucial for career advancement. According to AICTE, a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) holds parity with a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree, as they cover similar curriculum standards.

Equivalency in India: The AICTE recognizes that a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) is equivalent to an MBA, ensuring your qualification is highly respected and valued.

Comprehensive Curriculum: Both PGDM and MBA programs offer robust education in finance, marketing, human resources, operations, and strategy, validated by the AIU to meet industry standards. The PGDM curriculum is practical and industry-focused, incorporating real-world projects and case studies to prepare you for today 's business challenges.

Excellent Career Prospects: Graduates from both programs are equally valued by employers across various industries, giving you a competitive edge and opening up numerous career opportunities.
